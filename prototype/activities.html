Taniti is home to a vast array of activities for the whole family:

    - Relax on one of our white, sandy beaches
    - Snorkel the crystal blue coastline
    - Hike and zip-line through the rainforest
    - Explore the active volcano
    - Visit the history museum
    - Join a chartered fishing tour
    - Tour the island on a boat or bus or in a helicopter
    - Check-in at a pub, microbrewery, or dance club
    - See a movie or tour an art gallery
    - Visit an arcade or bowling alley
    - (Opening 2022!) Try out our new 9-hole golf course
